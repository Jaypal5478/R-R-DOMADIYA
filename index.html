FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the ITR filing deadline for FY 2025-26?
For FY 2025-26 (AY 2026-27), the ITR filing deadline for individuals and HUFs is 31st July 2025. For businesses requiring audit, it is 31st October 2025. We proactively remind all our clients well before deadlines.
Do I need to file GST returns if turnover is below threshold?
If you are registered under GST, you must file returns regardless of turnover. NIL returns are still mandatory. Businesses with turnover below ₹40L (goods) or ₹20L (services) don't need to register unless they opt-in or do inter-state supply.
What documents are needed for ITR filing?
You'll need: PAN Card, Aadhar Card, Form 16 (if salaried), bank statements, TDS certificates (Form 26AS), investment proofs for deductions, and details of other income. We guide you through the complete checklist after the initial consultation.
I received an income tax notice. What should I do?
Do not panic. Contact us immediately. We review the notice, identify the reason (mismatch in TDS, unreported income, etc.), prepare a proper response, and represent you before the Income Tax Department. Our litigation team has an excellent track record.
How much does it cost to file GST returns?
Our fees are transparent and competitive. GST return filing charges vary based on the number of invoices and type of business. We offer affordable monthly packages for small businesses and customized packages for larger clients. Contact us for a free quote.
Can you help with new business registration?
Absolutely! We assist with Proprietorship, Partnership Firm, Private Limited Company, LLP, and One Person Company (OPC) registration. We also handle GST registration, MSME/Udyam registration, Professional Tax enrollment, and all related compliances.
